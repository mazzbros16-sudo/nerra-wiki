## At A Glance

The Rift is one of the greatest mysteries in modern Nerra. While its exact nature remains poorly understood, it is widely associated with magical instability, strange phenomena, and many of the unanswered questions surrounding the Cataclysm.

References to the Rift become increasingly common in historical records following the Cataclysm, leading many scholars to believe the two events are connected. Whether the Rift caused the disaster, emerged because of it, or merely became visible during the chaos remains a subject of fierce debate.

Throughout Aedryn, the Rift is often spoken of with a mixture of fear and fascination. Scholars seek to understand it. Religious institutions argue over its significance. Ordinary people tend to avoid it entirely. Few things inspire more speculation.

## Account of a Survivor

_Taken from the journal of an unnamed merchant recovered near the eastern trade roads, date unknown._

> I first thought it was a storm.
> 
> The sky had gone wrong. Not dark. Wrong.
> 
> The clouds moved against the wind and the light seemed to bend around them. The horses noticed before any of us did. They refused to move forward.
> 
> Then we heard the sound.
> 
> It was like distant singing beneath water.
> 
> Not loud enough to understand. Just loud enough that you knew it was there.
> 
> One of the guards walked towards it. He said he could see lights between the trees.
> 
> We never saw him again.
> 
> We searched until dawn.
> 
> No tracks.
> 
> No blood.
> 
> No sign that he had ever existed beyond our memory.
> 
> We left before the sun rose fully.
> 
> I remember looking back once.
> 
> The trees were gone.
> 
> I swear upon every god that they were gone.
> 
> There was only mist.
> 
> I've crossed the Sunders. I've sailed through storms that should have sunk ships. I've stood in battle.
> 
> Nothing has ever frightened me like that place.
> 
> If that was truly the Rift, then I pray I never see it again.
