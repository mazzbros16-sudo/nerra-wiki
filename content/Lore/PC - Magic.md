## At A Glance

Magic occupies a complicated place within modern Aedryn. While it remains an undeniable part of everyday life, it is also feared by much of the population. To many ordinary citizens, magic is associated with instability, catastrophe and forces beyond mortal understanding.

Most people cannot meaningfully distinguish between the various forms of magic. Arcane practitioners, divine miracle workers, occult scholars and other wielders of supernatural power are often viewed with the same mixture of awe and suspicion. For many, the question is not where the power comes from, but whether it can be trusted.

Attitudes towards magic vary greatly throughout the continent. Some regions encourage its study and development, while others regard public displays of magic with deep unease. Regardless of where one travels, however, few subjects provoke stronger opinions than the use of supernatural power.
