## At A Glance

The Redentan are the dominant faction of the Stoneward Spine occupying the former Dwarven Holds following the collapse of the Dwarven Empire during the Smoke War.

Known for their curiosity, ingenuity and relentless pursuit of knowledge, the Redentan have become the foremost technological power in Aedryn. Their engineers continue to recover, study and improve upon the remnants of dwarven innovation, creating technologies unlike anything found elsewhere on the continent.

Although they maintain the smallest standing military among the major factions, their defensive capabilities are widely respected. The Redentan have little interest in conquest. Their focus is ensuring that they are never again forced to live under the rule of others.

Many outsiders view the Redentan as inventors and scholars. The Redentan themselves would likely prefer a simpler description.
