## At A Glance

The Folkmoor Creed is the dominant power of the Shattercoast and the faction responsible for overseeing much of the trade that flows throughout Aedryn and beyond. Born from cooperation between displaced Orcs and Wyrwood following the fall of the Dwarven Empire, the Creed built its influence not through conquest, but through commerce.

The people of the Creed value independence, initiative and practicality. Wealth, reputation and results often carry more weight than noble titles or political ideals. Their ports connect Aedryn to distant Shards, and few goods move across the continent without passing through their hands at some point.

The Creed maintains cordial relations with the other major factions, acting as the bridge between them through trade and diplomacy. While they rarely project military power openly, their influence is felt wherever merchants gather and deals are struck.

Many see the Creed as the engine that keeps the continent moving.

Others quietly wonder how much passes through their ports without anyone noticing.
