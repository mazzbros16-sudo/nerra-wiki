## At A Glance

The Velmor are the dominant political power of modern Aedryn and the driving force behind much of the continent's reconstruction following the Smoke War. Their influence extends far beyond their borders through trade, diplomacy and the actions of the [[PC - Crown Guard]].

Velmoran culture values ambition, productivity and civic responsibility. Many believe prosperity is built through hard work, strong institutions and a willingness to look towards the future rather than dwell upon the past.

As the largest and most influential faction on the continent, the Velmor often find themselves at the centre of Aedryn's political affairs. To their supporters they are the architects of stability. To their critics they are a nation increasingly convinced that it knows what is best for everyone else.
