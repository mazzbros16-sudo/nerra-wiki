## At A Glance

The Crown Guard serves as the primary law enforcement and stabilising force throughout Velmoran territory. Charged with maintaining order, protecting trade routes and responding to threats beyond the capacity of local authorities, their presence can be found across much of Aedryn.

While often viewed as a military organisation, the Crown Guard functions as both a policing force and a defensive institution. Their role is not to eliminate every danger, but to prevent instability from spreading and threatening the wider region.

The organisation is divided into several regional divisions, each adapted to the unique challenges of the territories they oversee. From the trade hubs of the the Shattercoast to the borders of the The Stoneward Spine, the Crown Guard acts as the visible extension of Velmoran authority.

Among ordinary citizens, the Crown Guard inspires a mixture of trust, respect and caution.
