## At A Glance

The Ellei are the dominant power within the Verdant Expanse and are composed primarily of elves, monkey folk and the many smaller societies that call the great forests home.

Of all the major factions in Aedryn, the Ellei are considered the most unified. They share a common belief in preserving the natural world and maintaining a balance between civilisation and the forces of nature.

The Ellei possess some of the continent's most accomplished mages and are renowned for their understanding of the Rift and its influence upon the world. While they are often viewed as isolationist by outsiders, the Ellei see themselves not as withdrawn, but as guardians of something worth protecting.

Their military is widely regarded as the best trained in Aedryn, though they favour defence and preservation over conquest and expansion.
