## At A Glance

Aedryn is one of the two major continents of Thyren and the primary setting of the Nerra campaign. Once dominated by the Dwarven Empire, the continent is now divided amongst several major powers, each shaped by the legacy of the Smoke War and the challenges of rebuilding a fractured world.

The continent is home to four major regions: the industrious Stoneward Spine, the wild Verdant Expanse, the trade-focused Shattercoast, and the prosperous heartlands of Velmora. Though united by geography, each region possesses its own cultures, beliefs and ambitions.

Despite decades of relative peace, the scars of history remain visible throughout Aedryn. Ancient ruins dot the landscape, tensions linger between old allies and rivals, and the consequences of the Cataclysm can still be felt across the continent.

## Overview

To most of its inhabitants, Aedryn is the centre of the world.

Its roads connect great cities and isolated villages alike. Merchant caravans cross its plains. Airships rise from its ports. Armies march across its borders. Generations have lived and died without ever seeing the lands that lie beyond its shores.

Modern Aedryn was forged in the aftermath of the Smoke War. The fall of the Dwarven Empire reshaped the political landscape, giving rise to the major factions that govern the continent today. While the war brought an end to imperial rule, it did not erase the divisions, memories and competing interests that continue to influence modern life.

The continent stretches from the dense forests and jungles of the Verdant Expanse to the towering peaks of the Stoneward Spine. Along its northern coast, the Shattercoast serves as a gateway to the wider world, while Velmora's cities drive much of the continent's trade, politics and development.

Most people view Aedryn as a land of opportunity. Others see it as a continent still learning how to live with the consequences of its past. ,Both are probably correct.
