The Shattercoast is where the edge of civilisation meets the edge of the world.

Stretching along Aedryn's northern coastline, the region is famous for its trade, shipping, salvage operations and wealth. Airships and merchant vessels constantly move through its ports, carrying goods from across Nerra.

Life on the Shattercoast is rarely predictable. The region is known for its independent spirit, unusual customs and willingness to embrace opportunities that other parts of Aedryn might consider reckless. Coin speaks loudly here, and practicality often matters more than tradition.

The people of the coast are heavily influenced by the Folkmoor Creed, a powerful alliance of merchants, traders, sailors and financiers that emerged following the Smoke War. Their influence extends far beyond the coastline itself.

The Shattercoast also sits closer to the Sunders than most people would prefer. Strange weather, unusual phenomena and unsettling sailor tales are all common parts of life. Most are likely exaggerations.
