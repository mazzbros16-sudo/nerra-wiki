The Stoneward Spine dominates the western horizon of Aedryn.

A vast mountain range of towering peaks, deep valleys and sprawling underground holds, it was once the heartland of the dwarven empire that ruled much of the continent during the Smoke War. Even today, the mountains remain shaped by that legacy.

Much of the Spine is now inhabited by the Redentan, whose settlements and industries have emerged amongst the remnants of dwarven infrastructure. Trade, engineering and technological innovation play a significant role throughout the region, with many of Aedryn's most advanced industries tracing their origins back to the mountains.

Despite ongoing rebuilding efforts, the Spine remains a place of mixed emotions throughout the continent. Some see it as a symbol of liberation and progress. Others see it as a reminder of occupation, war and old wounds that have never fully healed.

Travellers passing through the region will encounter bustling industrial settlements, ancient stone roads, abandoned fortifications and countless reminders of the conflict that shaped modern Aedryn.
