The Verdant Expanse is one of the oldest and least understood regions of Aedryn.

Vast forests, dense jungles and ancient wilderness stretch across much of the region, swallowing roads, ruins and entire settlements beneath layers of growth. Many places within the Expanse have remained untouched for generations, while others are known only through stories and half-forgotten maps.

The region is home to many elves, vanara and lizardfolk, though numerous smaller communities exist hidden amongst the wilderness. Outsiders are often treated cautiously, particularly in the deeper reaches of the Expanse, where trust is earned slowly and traditions run deep.

The people of the Expanse tend to view the outside world with a degree of scepticism. They have watched empires rise and fall, wars come and go, and many believe civilisation has a habit of creating its own problems.

Travellers often speak of strange ruins hidden beneath the trees, forgotten roads reclaimed by nature, and creatures older than recorded history moving through the deepest wilderness.

Whether such stories are true is another matter entirely.
