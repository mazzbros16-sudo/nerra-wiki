If Aedryn has a heart, it beats within Velmora.

Stretching across much of central Aedryn, Velmora is the largest political power on the continent and the region most heavily associated with rebuilding following the Smoke War. Its roads are maintained, its cities are growing, and its influence can be felt almost everywhere.  The commanding power of the region are the Velmor.

The people of Velmora value hard work, ambition and perseverance. Many believe that civilisation survives because ordinary people continue getting up every morning and doing what needs to be done. This mentality helped shape much of the modern continent after the war. 

Yet beneath the prosperity lies tension. The war may have ended, but few have forgotten it. Veterans are common throughout the region, political disagreements simmer beneath the surface, and the presence of the Crown Guard serves as a constant reminder that peace is not something to be taken for granted.
