## At A Glance

Needleberry is a small rural town located in southern Aedryn, near the foothills of the Stoneward Spine. Known for its muddy roads, ageing buildings and hardworking residents, it serves as a waypoint for travellers moving between the frontier settlements and the wider world.

Though far from the centres of political power, Needleberry remains an important stop for merchants, labourers and adventurers travelling through the region.

## Overview

Few people set out to visit Needleberry. Fewer still stay longer than they need to.

Nestled amongst rolling hills and muddy roads on the fringes of civilisation, Needleberry is the sort of place travellers pass through on their way somewhere more important. The town is small, weathered and unremarkable by most maps. Its timber buildings lean slightly with age, its roads become thick with mud after even the lightest rain, and many of its fences seem to have been repaired so many times that nobody remembers what the original timber looked like.

Needleberry has a reputation amongst travellers as a place where caution and kindness exist side by side. Most people are willing to lend a hand if someone is in need, though they would probably recommend keeping one hand near their coin purse while doing so. The town sits far enough from major population centres that law and order can occasionally become flexible, particularly when the nearest authority is days away.

At the heart of the town sits The Bent Needle, a well-loved tavern that serves as Needleberry's unofficial gathering place. If there is news to be heard, work to be found, or trouble brewing, it will eventually find its way through the tavern doors.

To most of Aedryn, Needleberry is forgettable. To the people who call it home, it is simply home.
